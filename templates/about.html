ABOUT THE APP



This app was started 3 days prior to the igniteCS Expo (September 11th Registration), by Brian Wang & Devin Wang.
Part of the reason why we built this is for users to easily encrypt files for employees within large companies.
With a new home-made encryption method, it will be difficult for others to "hack" or decrypt these files without a key.



A download link will be given, and users can download the encrypted file.
They can then decrypt this file back here with a key.

ABOUT THE APP



This app was started on September 11th of 2022 by Brian Wang & Devin Wang.
Part of the reason why we built this is for users to easily encrypt files for employees within large companies.
With a new home-made encryption method, it will be difficult for others to "hack" or decrypt these files without a key.



A user can encrypt their files with our home-made algorithm within this site using a password.
Then, they can decrypt the file with the same password.
